Status pages

  • See what services are running well and which are reporting issues on the Status page. If a problem is showing here, Infra has already received an alert about it.

  • Our ASF Infrastructure Reporting Dashboard has a variety of live reports, including uptime statistics, website source scans, and more.

  • To view download stats for your project, visit the download stats page.

  • Review how Infra triages incident reports and service requests, and their response-time goals.

  • The Reporter wizard provides a wide range of interesting information about Apache projects, and can help PMCs assemble their quarterly Board reports.

  • Abuse and connectivity issues: ABC lets you check whether your IP address has been blocked for a violation of one of the ASF rules of use, such as more than a set number of pageviews per hour, and provides guidance about what you should to to get the IP address unblocked.
