Infra News

January 13, 2025: A new policy governing third-party content on project websites comes into effect March 1. The goal is to bring websites into compliance with The ASF's privacy policies. Read about the new policy here.

October 22, 2024: We are seeing a sharp increase in 'phishing attempts', fake emails pretending to come from various ASF groups and committees. Infra has some anti-phishing advice to help you avoid falling for these fake messages.

Services and Tools

Roundtables

Infra holds Roundtable meetings most months to discuss infrastructure development and challenges. Participants are welcome to bring their own concerns, questions, and suggestions.

The next Roundtable will be Wednesday, March 5, 2025, time TBD. This is a repeat of the February 5 presentation on creation, review and management of custom GitHub Actions that projects can use in their compile and build processes, and the self-serve way to add custom GitHub Actions to the GHA Allowlist. This second presentation is for the convenience of participants in Asia and Australia.

Inside Infra

Infra publishes a monthly newsletter, "Inside Infra", on our blog and also emails it to Members and committers who have subscribed to the users@infra.apache.org email list. This is a handy way to catch up on Infrastructure developments, policy changes, and new resources.
