Welcome to the website of Infra, the Apache Infrastructure Team!

Serving the technical needs of The Apache Software Foundation and its projects

Looking for Help?

The Apache Infrastructure team, or Infra, supports the day-to-day operations of the technical aspects of the foundation and its projects. If you have questions or require support with email, code repositories, virtual machines, web sites, issue trackers and more, we are here to help!

Services and Tools

Infra supports services for the ASF and its projects, including GitBox, Jira, Confluence, Jenkins, and Buildbot. We have an extensive toolbox that contains both home-brewed solutions and "off the shelf" offerings. Feel free to check them out.

Self-serve

Use our self-serve features to perform tasks ranging from requesting a Jira account to creating a mailing list, without having to involve the Infra team.

Roundtables

Infra holds Roundtable meetings most months to discuss infrastructure development and challenges. Participants are welcome to bring their own concerns, questions, and suggestions. This is a great way to get a quick answer to a problem or suggest a useful feature.

Inside Infra

Infra publishes a monthly newsletter, "Inside Infra", on our blog and also emails it to Members and committers who have subscribed to the users@infra.apache.org email list. This is a handy way to catch up on Infrastructure developments, policy changes, and new resources.

News from Infra

May 10, 2024: Apache Paste, a tool for sharing code snippets and similar material, is no longer available.

April 12, 2024: the ASF Infrastructure Reporting Dashboard provides a collection of reports on the overall health and activity of the infrastructure at the ASF. These reports can be helpful in understanding the status of all the ASF 'under the hood' resources, and in assessing the resource cost of some activities, like build processes. Learn more.
