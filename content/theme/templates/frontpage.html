Infra News

April 2, 2025: If you cannot access one or more ASF repositories or services, your IP address may have been blocked. Before contacting Infra, review Abuse and Connectivity Issues at the ASF (ABC). The page lists the common overuse (or abuse) issues that affect our site's work and cause an automatic IP address block, and recommends steps to get such a block removed.

January 13, 2025: A new policy governing third-party content on project websites came into effect March 1. The goal is to bring websites into compliance with The ASF's privacy policies. Read about the new policy here.

Inside Infra

Infra publishes a monthly newsletter, "Inside Infra", on our blog and also emails it to Members and committers who have subscribed to the users@infra.apache.org email list. This is a handy way to catch up on Infrastructure developments, policy changes, and new resources.
